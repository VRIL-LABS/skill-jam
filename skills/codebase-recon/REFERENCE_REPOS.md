## How to Use This File

1. **Before implementing** a feature, module, or system: scan the relevant tier-1 entries to understand dominant patterns.
2. **During implementation**: cross-reference specific files listed under "Key files/patterns to study."
3. **When debugging**: search the Awareness tier for known edge cases documented by the community.
4. **Adding entries manually**: follow the template below to maintain consistent structure.
